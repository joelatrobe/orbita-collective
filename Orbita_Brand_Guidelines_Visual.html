Voice & tone

Use

  • reinvent
  • own / usable
  • inside-out
  • simple, fresh, impact
  • British English (organise, behaviour)
  • Active voice, contractions

Avoid

  • synergize, leverage, paradigm
  • best-in-class, world-class
  • cutting-edge, disruptive
  • deep dive, circle back
  • Holistic, frameworks for their own sake
  • Multiple typefaces
