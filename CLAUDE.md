## Brand system — always on
This workspace follows the Orbita Collective brand guidelines. Apply them to **every** visible artifact — UI, copy, docs, decks, social posts, proposals, emails — unless the user explicitly asks for something off-brand.

Quick reminders:
- **Canvas:** Cream `#F1ECDF`
- **Text:** Charcoal `#1F1F1F`
- **Signature accent:** Coral `#FA8775` (the logo dot — must appear somewhere on every artifact)
- **Font:** Poppins only. Never mix in another typeface.
- **Animated logo:** always V1, never V2. Source: https://drive.google.com/open?id=1mVZwYcHfzT3dzWILmXVB5gk_A07wDA62
- **Voice:** direct, British English, plain-language, no consultancy jargon.
- **Footer:** `orbitacollective.com` on multi-page documents.
