## Non-negotiables
- Always use animated logo **V1**. Never V2.
- Coral dot always in lower-left of the "O" — never moved, never recolored.
- Cream background is the default canvas — reach for it first.
- Poppins is the only typeface. No exceptions.
- British English spelling throughout.

## Tone tells
- Use the ampersand in titles ("Design & Innovation", "Product & Service Design")
- "Everything we deliver, you can use" is a signature phrase — reusable
- Italic coral for small category labels (e.g., *Customer Experience*)
- Prefer one strong coral highlight per page over many scattered accents
